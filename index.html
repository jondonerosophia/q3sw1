Characters
Information


Musa is the Fairy of Music and She manipulates sound waves to form her attacks, and she also writes and performs her own music.



Tecna is the Fairy of Technology. She is a rational thinker and the team's science expert.



Flora is the sensitive and gentle Fairy of Nature. She cares deeply for plants and gets her powers from them.


Bloom is the Fairy of the Dragon Flame and the leader of the Winx Club. Bloom uses fire-based attacks and is the most powerful fairy on the team.


Aisha is the Fairy of Waves. She is a take-charge athlete who joins the team in the final special, "The Shadow Phoenix".


Roxy is the tomboyish and shy Fairy of Animals. She is an emotional animal lover introduced in Season 4.



Stella is the artistic and creative Fairy of the Shining Sun. Stella uses light-based attacks and is the comic relief of the Winx team.
